Design history

In order to get to know a little about me, first you would have to know a little about WHY i'm a designer.

i started design in order to improve on my comic. from there i developed a few skills in the following programmes

  • Manga Studio / Clip Studio
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe InDesign

After that i ended up working at Nuturn . The next step on my designer journey took me to 2020 Marketing where i nurtured my skills enough to become a graphic head.

At the moment i work independently as i try to master the fine art of WEBSITE DESIGN! wish me luck
